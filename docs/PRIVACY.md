# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: October 2025

## Introduction

We operate the TweetWeb application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information when you use our App.

## Our Commitment to Privacy

TweetWeb is designed with privacy as a core principle. We utilize a decentralized architecture built on IPFS (InterPlanetary File System) to ensure that your data is not stored in centralized servers controlled by us.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our App for you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### 1. Personal Data

While using our App, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including but not limited to:

- **Username**: For account identification within the platform
- **Email Address**: For account recovery and important notifications (optional)
- **Profile Information**: Bio, avatar, and other profile customization data

**Important**: All personal data you provide is stored on IPFS in a decentralized manner. We do not maintain centralized databases of user information.

#### 2. Usage Data

We may collect information on how the App is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include:

- Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address)
- Browser type and version
- Pages of our App that you visit
- Time and date of your visit
- Time spent on pages
- Other diagnostic data

**Note**: Usage data is collected locally and not transmitted to central servers. It is used solely for improving app performance and user experience.

#### 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our App and hold certain information:

- **Session Cookies**: To operate our service
- **Preference Cookies**: To remember your preferences and settings
- **Security Cookies**: To authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use

**You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.**

## How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

### Primary Uses

- **To provide and maintain our App**: Core functionality and features
- **To notify you about changes**: Important updates to our App
- **To enable participation**: Interactive features when you choose to use them
- **To provide customer support**: Responding to your requests and inquiries
- **To gather analysis**: Valuable information to improve our App
- **To monitor usage**: Understanding how the App is used
- **To detect and prevent issues**: Technical problems and security threats

### Data Storage

- **Decentralized Storage**: All content (posts, images, videos) is stored on IPFS
- **No Central Database**: We do not maintain a centralized database of your posts or content
- **Peer-to-Peer**: Content is distributed across the IPFS network
- **User Control**: You control your content through IPFS Content Identifiers (CIDs)

## Data Sharing

### We Do NOT:

- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Store your content on centralized servers
- ❌ Track your activity across other websites
- ❌ Create user profiles for marketing purposes

### We MAY share data:

- ✅ With your explicit consent
- ✅ To comply with legal obligations
- ✅ To protect our rights and safety
- ✅ Via IPFS network (as part of the decentralized architecture)

## Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### Our Security Measures

- **Decentralized Architecture**: No single point of failure
- **IPFS Content Addressing**: Content-based addressing for integrity
- **Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted in transit
- **Automatic Cleanup**: Temporary files are automatically deleted
- **File Size Limits**: Protection against abuse (4GB videos, 500MB archives)

### Your Responsibility

While we strive to protect your data:

- 🔒 **Do not share sensitive personal information** publicly on the App
- 🔒 **Use strong passwords** and keep them secure
- 🔒 **Be cautious** about what you post, as decentralized data is difficult to delete completely
- 🔒 **Understand IPFS**: Once content is on IPFS, it may be replicated by other nodes

## Data Retention

### Content You Create

- **Posts and Media**: Stored on IPFS indefinitely (unless you delete them)
- **IPFS Characteristics**: Data on IPFS is content-addressed and may persist even after deletion attempts
- **User Control**: You can delete posts from your feed, but cannot guarantee removal from all IPFS nodes

### Temporary Data

- **Upload Temporary Files**: Deleted immediately after processing
- **Video Conversion Files**: Cleaned up after 1 hour or upon completion
- **Session Data**: Cleared when you log out

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data (subject to IPFS limitations)
- **Restriction**: Request limitation of data processing
- **Portability**: Request transfer of your data
- **Objection**: Object to our processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the support channels.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Third-Party Services

### IPFS Network

Our App uses IPFS for decentralized storage. Content you upload is distributed across the IPFS network and may be:

- Replicated by other IPFS nodes
- Accessible by anyone with the content identifier (CID)
- Difficult to remove once propagated
